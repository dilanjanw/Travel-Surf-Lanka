Prepared itineraries

Practical Sri Lanka routes with real activities and destinations.

These plans are built for actual road times, realistic day pacing, and a better travel flow. They can be adjusted for surf season, weather, and your preferred pace.

5 Days — South Coast Surf Starter

Weligama • Mirissa • Galle Fort • Unawatuna

Day-by-day plan
  1. Day 1: Airport pickup, transfer to Weligama, beach check-in, sunset walk.
  2. Day 2: Beginner surf lesson in Weligama, board fitting, sunset at Mirissa.
  3. Day 3: Galle Fort walking tour, lighthouse photos, dinner in the old town.
  4. Day 4: Unawatuna beach time, optional snorkelling, turtle hatchery stop.
  5. Day 5: Morning surf or coffee stop, then departure or extension.

7 Days — South Coast & Wildlife

Galle • Mirissa • Udawalawe • Ella

Day-by-day plan
  1. Day 1: Arrival, coastal transfer to Galle area, relaxed dinner.
  2. Day 2: Galle Fort, local market stop, sunset drive to Mirissa.
  3. Day 3: Whale watching from Mirissa in season, beach afternoon, coconut hill viewpoint.
  4. Day 4: Drive to Udawalawe for an evening safari and elephant spotting.
  5. Day 5: Morning safari option, then transfer to Ella with waterfall stops.
  6. Day 6: Nine Arch Bridge, Little Adam's Peak, tea at a hillside café.
  7. Day 7: Scenic return or continue to the hill country.

10 Days — Culture, Hills & Coast

Sigiriya • Dambulla • Kandy • Nuwara Eliya • Ella • South Coast

Day-by-day plan
  1. Day 1: Arrival, transfer toward Dambulla or Sigiriya region.
  2. Day 2: Sigiriya Rock Fortress climb and village lunch.
  3. Day 3: Dambulla Cave Temple and spice garden stop on the way to Kandy.
  4. Day 4: Kandy city walk, lake area, Temple of the Tooth, evening cultural show.
  5. Day 5: Tea country drive to Nuwara Eliya, tea factory visit, Gregory Lake area.
  6. Day 6: Scenic transfer to Ella, waterfalls, viewpoints, and bridge photos.
  7. Day 7: Free morning in Ella, then move to the south coast.
  8. Day 8: Weligama surf lesson and beach time.
  9. Day 9: Mirissa or Unawatuna day, seafood lunch, sunset stop.
  10. Day 10: Airport transfer or add an extra coast day.

12 Days — Complete Sri Lanka Loop

Coast • Surf • Wildlife • Tea Country • Ancient sites

Day-by-day plan
  1. Day 1: Arrival and transfer to the southern coast.
  2. Day 2: Relaxed beach day and local seafood dinner.
  3. Day 3: Surf session in Weligama with board rental and coaching.
  4. Day 4: Galle Fort, artisan shops, and slow coastal lunch.
  5. Day 5: Udawalawe safari and elephant viewing.
  6. Day 6: Transfer to Ella, Ravana Falls, and viewpoint stops.
  7. Day 7: Tea country day in Haputale with a plantation walk.
  8. Day 8: Nuwara Eliya drive and tea factory visit.
  9. Day 9: Kandy, lake walk, and temple visit.
  10. Day 10: Sigiriya and Dambulla.
  11. Day 11: Return to the south coast for a final surf or beach day.
  12. Day 12: Departure transfer.

Surf

Surf packages for beginner, intermediate, and advanced guests.

Built around Sri Lanka's south-coast surf rhythm, these packages can be adapted for a single beach stay, a mixed surf-and-tour itinerary, or an east-coast season trip.

Beginner Surf Lite

1 lesson a day

For first-timers and relaxed beach holidays.

  • One surf lesson per day
  • Board rentals included
  • Flexible lesson times

Intermediate Surf Course

2 lessons a day
