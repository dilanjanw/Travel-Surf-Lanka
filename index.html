Tour itineraries

Four Sri Lanka routes, organized by duration and travel flow.

Each itinerary is arranged for smooth road time, clear overnight stops, and a balanced mix of culture, nature, safari, beaches, and adventure.

8 Days — Kandy, Hill Country & South Coast

Negombo • Kandy • Nuwara Eliya • Ella • Yala • Weligama • Galle • Airport

Day-by-day plan
  1. Day 1: Negombo / Airport to Kandy (3 hrs) — Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage. Overnight in Kandy.
  2. Day 2: Kandy Temple of the Tooth at ceremony time (09:30), city viewpoint, and a 1.5 km Udawattakele forest trek. Overnight in Kandy.
  3. Day 3: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya (3 hrs) — Ambuluwawa Tower by tuk-tuk, Kithulgala white-water rafting, and St. Clair’s & Devon waterfalls. Overnight in Nuwara Eliya.
  4. Day 4: Nuwara Eliya to Ella (2.5 hrs) — Moon Plains by jeep, tea factory visit, explore Little England, and take the noon train to Ella. Overnight in Ella.
  5. Day 5: Little Adam’s Peak or Ella Rock, zip line, Ravana pool club with lunch, Nine Arch Bridge, then drive to Yala. Overnight in Yala.
  6. Day 6: Yala to Weligama / Mirissa (3 hrs) — Morning safari, Blue Beach Island visit, Mirissa Beach, surf, and Coconut Tree Hill for sunset. Overnight in Weligama.
  7. Day 7: Weligama leisure, Unawatuna Turtle Point, and Galle Dutch Fort for sunset. Overnight in Weligama.
  8. Day 8: Weligama to Airport (2.5 hrs) — Leisure, then drive to the airport and goodbye from the island.

12 Days — Culture, Wildlife & Coast

Negombo • Sigiriya • Riverston • Kandy • Nuwara Eliya • Ella • Tangalle • Unawatuna • Airport

Day-by-day plan
  1. Day 1: Negombo / Airport to Sigiriya (3 hrs) — Sigiriya village tour with local lunch.
  2. Day 2: Sigiriya — Pidurangala Rock, Minneriya safari for elephants, and Ayurvedic massage.
  3. Day 3: Sigiriya to Riverston (3 hrs) — Dambulla vegetable market, Cave Temple, Thelgamu Oya natural pools, and a homestay dinner with your hosts.
  4. Day 4: Riverston to Kandy (3 hrs) — Manigala mountain trekking (3.5 hrs), Pitawala Pathana mini World’s End, and Bambarakiriella waterfall.
  5. Day 5: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya (3 hrs) — Temple of the Tooth, city viewpoint, Kithulgala white-water rafting, and St. Clair’s & Devon waterfalls.
  6. Day 6: Nuwara Eliya to Ella — Moon Plains by jeep, tea factory visit, noon train to Ella, and Nine Arch Bridge.
  7. Day 7: Ella to Tangalle / Hiriketiya (3 hrs) — Little Adam’s Peak, zip line, Ravana waterfall, and beach leisure.
  8. Day 8: Tangalle — Leisure and Hummanaya blowhole.
  9. Day 9: Tangalle — Visit nearby beaches and Turtle Point.
  10. Day 10: Tangalle to Unawatuna (2 hrs) — Mirissa Beach, Coconut Tree Hill, Weligama Beach, surf, and swim with turtles in Unawatuna.
  11. Day 12: Unawatuna to Airport (2 hrs) — Madu River boat safari, then drive to the airport and goodbye from the island.

13 Days — Sigiriya, Trincomalee & Hill Country

Negombo • Sigiriya • Trincomalee • Kandy • Nuwara Eliya • Ella • Arugam Bay

Day-by-day plan
  1. Day 1: Negombo / Airport to Sigiriya (3 hrs) — Sigiriya village tour.
  2. Day 2: Sigiriya — Morning safari in Minneriya and Pidurangala Rock.
  3. Day 3: Sigiriya to Trincomalee (1.5 hrs) — Kayaking in Kanthale Lake.
  4. Day 4: Trincomalee — Leisure.
  5. Day 5: Trincomalee — Whale and dolphin watching, plus a visit to Koneswaram Hindu Temple.
  6. Day 6: Trincomalee to Kandy (4 hrs) — Spice garden visit and Kandy city viewpoint.
  7. Day 7: Kandy — Peradeniya Botanical Garden, gem and wood carving shops, and a cultural dance show.
  8. Day 8: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya — Kithulgala white-water rafting, St. Clair’s & Devon waterfalls, city time, and Gregory Lake.
  9. Day 9: Nuwara Eliya — Horton Plains and leisure.
  10. Day 10: Nuwara Eliya to Ella — Morning train to Ella, Nine Arch Bridge, and Kombukara natural pools for a swim.
  11. Day 12: Ella — Ella Rock or Little Adam’s Peak, plus Ravana pool club with lunch.
  12. Day 13: Ella to Arugam Bay (3.5 hrs) — Drive to Arugam Bay and drop off.

15 Days — Wildlife, Hill Country & Surf

Negombo • Wilpattu • Sigiriya • Riverston • Kandy • Nuwara Eliya • Ella • Udawalawe • Weligama • Hikkaduwa • Airport

Day-by-day plan
  1. Day 1: Negombo / Airport to Wilpattu (3 hrs) — Wilpattu afternoon safari.
  2. Day 2: Wilpattu to Sigiriya — Sigiriya village tour with local lunch, and Pidurangala Rock or Sigiriya Lion Rock for sunset.
  3. Day 3: Sigiriya — Ancient city of Polonnaruwa and Minneriya elephant safari.
  4. Day 4: Sigiriya to Riverston (3 hrs) — Dambulla vegetable market, Cave Temple, Thelgamu Oya natural pools, and a homestay dinner.
  5. Day 5: Riverston to Kandy (3 hrs) — Manigala mountain trekking (3.5 hrs), Pitawala Pathana mini World’s End, and Bambarakiriella waterfall.
  6. Day 6: Kandy — Temple of the Tooth, Udawattakele forest trekking, city tour and viewpoint, plus a cultural dance show.
  7. Day 7: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya — Kithulgala white-water rafting, St. Clair’s & Devon waterfalls, city time, and Gregory Lake.
  8. Day 8: Nuwara Eliya to Ella (2.5 hrs) — Horton Plains, train from Ohiya to Ella, and Nine Arch Bridge.
  9. Day 9: Ella — Little Adam’s Peak or Ella Rock, zip line, Ravana pool club with lunch, and a cookery class followed by dinner.
  10. Day 10: Ella to Udawalawe (2 hrs) — Elephant Transit Home milk feeding and Udawalawe National Park safari.
  11. Day 11: Udawalawe to Weligama (2 hrs) — Leisure.
  12. Day 12: Weligama — Surf session, leisure, and an afternoon surf session.
  13. Day 13: Weligama — Whale watching and leisure.
  14. Day 14: Weligama to Hikkaduwa (1.5 hrs) — Turtle hatchery, Turtle Beach, and Galle Dutch Fort.
  15. Day 15: Hikkaduwa to Airport (2 hrs) — Leisure, then drive to the airport.
